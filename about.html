About the Sensei

My name is Chris, aka Frozen on KGS. I am currently AGA 6d, KGS 7d. I've been teaching go online as well as in person at the Go Orlando club in Orlando Florida, since 2014. Teaching go full time has been a dream of mine since 2009, and the success of the first post in reddit, and subsequent streams I've done after it, have allowed me to make it a reality.

About the class

Currently I offer a class on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday nights from 8pm to 10pm Est. The class functions best with around 7 people. The classes are mainly targeted at SDK players trying to make the push to Dan level, but Wednesday class is open to DDK players. On average, students that start with me around 9-7k are typically 3-1k within 3-6 months.

One to one lessons

Apart from the group classes I also have time slots open for 1 on 1 lessons. Slots for one on one lessons are usually booked for 1 to 1 and 1/2 hours. Available times ranging from 11am Est to 6pm Est. I am free every day of the week.
